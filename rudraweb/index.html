About Us

Welcome to RUDRA - Resourceful Unit for Data Research and Analytics, a dynamic college club dedicated to empowering students with data skills and fostering a community passionate about data research and analytics.

At RUDRA, we align with RV University's mission to prepare students for the VUCA (Volatility, Uncertainty, Complexity, and Ambiguity) world by focusing on problem-solving, critical thinking, innovation, creativity, communication, entrepreneurship, and data science.

As RV University's programmes offer an opportunity for students to work closely with industries, government policymakers, researchers, think tanks, and global organizations, with a focus on imparting 21st-century skills through experiential, holistic learning, we offer our small but significant support in adopting a model of pedagogy to build impressive portfolios and emphasizes interdisciplinary studies, internships, and student exchange programs.

RUDRA, as a society, aims to promote knowledge of Data Analytics and Data Science among RVU students, ensuring they become proficient Data Scientists. Our initiatives include excellence in Data Research and Analytics, knowledge sharing, collaboration, skill development, community building, innovation, technological advancement, and research on new advancements and industry requirements.
